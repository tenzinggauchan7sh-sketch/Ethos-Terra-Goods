Research & Design Analysis

Ethos Terra Goods VS. industry top players

Introduction

This research looks at the design and layout of existing websites to understand common industry standards, reccuring design patterns, and areas where improvements can be made. The findings directly shaped the design of Ethos Terra Goods, ensure the site aligns with user expectations while delivering a clearer, more approachable, and user-friendly experince that emphasize sustainability.

Side by Side Comparision

Design Comparision

Key Similarities

  1. Used Similar type of footer from Islington College
  2. Used Similar container to hold contents
  3. Grid-based product display layout
  4. High-quality product and lifestyle like bamboo home ware

Key Differences

  1. ETG uses lighter, more approachable header
  2. Simplified navigation compared to competitors
  3. Promotional coneter are minimized
  4. Strong visual hierarchy on homepage

Key Improvement Made

  • Improved color contrast for readability
  • Clear section hierarchy
  • Reduced visual chaos
  • Eliminated unecessary effect and icons

Conclusion

This comparative analysis demonstrate that the design of Ethos Terra Good is not random. It is designed with sustanibility, simplicity and better services on mind. The design aim for eco-lovers, while firmly establishing a credit and appealing sustainable brand identity.
